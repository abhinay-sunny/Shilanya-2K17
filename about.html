About Our College


JNTU Anantapur College of Engineering, Kalikiri was established in the year 2013 and works as constituent college for JNTU Anantapur. This college functions with permanent buildings accommodating the Administrative block, Academic block, Laboratories, Library and the Hostel blocks. Fortified with traditional values and dedicated spirit of the staff and the students, we strongly believe it would rise up to any occasion or meet any challenge confidently.

This college has a vast campus sprawling over an area of 110 acres, in a peaceful atmosphere away from the dust and din of the town. The college is just 75km away from Tirupati & Chittoor and is well connected to the cities like Chennai and Bengaluru. With this geographical arena, it joins hands with the JNT University, Anantapur to fulfil the technical need of our fast-developing country with highly professional and well trained graduates.

The vision of college is to produce quality technical graduates and better leaders of the society. With this vision, it is bound to go forward crossing all the possible hurdles in development.
