Come, join hands and be the part of our joy and memories. Now is the time to stretch your wings and fly high.

Shilanya is the annual technical festival of Civil Engineering department of Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Anantapur College of Engineering, Kalikiri, Andhra Pradesh. Being the first technical fest of Civil Engineering department in the newly established constituent of JNTU Anantapur, Shilanya 2K17 is bound to enrich the builders of this nation with technology.

Technology is a part of everyday life. Modern societies have very much connected to the technology as food and oxygen. Everything we do throughout the day is intertwined with gadgets of various forms and features developed with trending technologies. Tech has evolved as a platform for creators, thinkers and professional students to express themselves and bring to life their dreams and ideas.

These are the thoughts that we hope drive us forward for years to make Shilanya bigger, better and spectacular. We believe that with your coordination and support, Shilanya will one day gradually evolve as one of the recognised technical fests of our country.
